Environmental Management & Statutory Compliances

We assist industries in meeting statutory compliance requirements under various environmental laws and guidelines. From conducting detailed environmental audits to preparing statutory reports and returns, our expert team ensures your organization remains fully compliant with CPCB, SPCB, and MOEF regulations. This not only avoids legal penalties but also improves environmental performance and sustainability practices.

Audits & Assessments

  • ✅ Environmental Audit – A systematic and documented process to assess the environmental performance of your facility. It covers air, water, waste, and energy management practices, evaluates compliance with CPCB/SPCB guidelines, and identifies potential environmental risks. Provides a roadmap for achieving sustainability targets and reducing liabilities.
  • ✅ Hazardous Waste Audit – Focused on identifying, quantifying, and evaluating hazardous waste generation sources. Includes proper storage, labeling, transport, treatment, and disposal practices in compliance with the Hazardous & Other Waste (Management & Transboundary Movement) Rules, 2016. Ensures safe handling, minimizes environmental damage, and helps industries maintain cradle-to-grave accountability.
  • ✅ Water Audit – A complete assessment of water intake, consumption, recycling, and discharge across all industrial processes. Identifies opportunities for water conservation, rainwater harvesting, reuse/recycling, and compliance with zero liquid discharge (ZLD) norms. Helps industries save on costs, reduce dependency on freshwater sources, and improve long-term water sustainability.
  • ✅ Energy Audit – A detailed study of power consumption patterns, process efficiency, and energy losses. Evaluates the performance of motors, compressors, boilers, lighting systems, and HVAC units. Suggests energy conservation measures (ECMs) such as retrofitting, automation, renewable energy adoption, and load optimization. Leads to cost savings, reduced carbon footprint, and improved operational efficiency.

Compliance Documentation

  • ✅ Six Monthly Compliance Reports – Regular reporting to SPCB/MOEF on the status of environmental clearance conditions.
  • ✅ Environment Statement Report (Form V) – Annual reporting of environmental performance, energy/water conservation, and pollution control measures under the EPA 1986.
  • ✅ Annual Hazardous Waste Returns (Form 4) – Submission of yearly details on hazardous waste generation, storage, treatment, and disposal.
  • ✅ Hazardous Waste Manifest (Form 10) – Documentation required for safe transportation of hazardous waste, ensuring tracking from source to disposal site.
  • ✅ Annual E-Waste Returns (Form 3) – Filing reports on e-waste generated, collected, stored, and recycled/disposed as per E-Waste Rules, 2016.
  • ✅ Biomedical Waste Returns (Form 4) – Annual reporting of biomedical waste handling, treatment, and disposal for healthcare facilities as per BMW Rules, 2016.
  • ✅ Battery Waste Returns – Reporting of used lead-acid battery collection, recycling, and disposal in compliance with Battery Waste Management Rules, 2020.
