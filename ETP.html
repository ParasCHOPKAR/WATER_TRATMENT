Effluent Treatment Plant
(ETP)

Technologies We Offer:

  • ✔ MBBR: Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR) Technology: Our Effluent Treatment Plant incorporates MBBR technology, an innovative biological treatment process that uses suspended biofilm carriers to enhance the microbial activity
  • ✔ SBR: Sequential Batch Reactor (SBR) Technology: With the Sequential Batch Reactor (SBR) technology integrated into our Effluent Treatment Plant, you benefit from a batch-operated system that provides excellent treatment efficiency
  • ✔ MBR: Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) Technology: Our Effluent Treatment Plant features advanced Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) technology, which combines the biological treatment process with membrane filtration.
  • ✔ FAB: Fixed Activated Sludge (FAB) Technology: The Effluent Treatment Plant also employs the Fixed Activated Sludge (FAB) technology, which facilitates the growth of microorganisms on a specially designed fixed media.

Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR)

The MBBR process uses specially designed biofilm carriers suspended in wastewater. Microorganisms grow on these carriers, breaking down organic pollutants efficiently. It requires less space and ensures stable treatment performance even under variable loads.

  • ✔ Compact & cost-effective
  • ✔ Handles shock loads efficiently
  • ✔ Low sludge generation
  • ✔ Minimal operator attention required

Membrane Bio Reactor (MBR)

MBR combines biological treatment with membrane filtration (UF/MF membranes). It produces crystal-clear, pathogen-free water suitable for reuse in gardening, cooling, or industrial purposes.

  • ✔ High-quality effluent (almost drinking standard)
  • ✔ Compact footprint
  • ✔ Eliminates need for secondary clarification
  • ✔ Ideal for water reuse applications

Sequential Batch Reactor (SBR)

The SBR is a fill-and-draw activated sludge system. Treatment occurs in batches through stages: fill, aeration, settling, decanting, and sludge removal. It’s highly flexible and efficient in nutrient removal.

  • ✔ Flexible operation for varying loads
  • ✔ Excellent nutrient (N & P) removal
  • ✔ Suitable for medium to large installations
  • ✔ Automated cycle operation

Fixed Activated Sludge (FAB) Technology

The FAB process facilitates the growth of microorganisms on specially designed fixed media. This fixed film media increases the surface area for microbial growth, enhancing the breakdown of organic pollutants and improving overall treatment efficiency.

  • ✔ High treatment efficiency
  • ✔ Compact & modular design
  • ✔ Stable under varying load conditions
  • ✔ Reduced sludge generation
