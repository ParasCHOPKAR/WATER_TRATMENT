Our Products & Solutions

We provide innovative and sustainable solutions to meet your industrial and environmental needs.

Common Sewage Treatment Plants(CSTP)

Advanced sewage treatment solutions designed for residential and industrial needs. Ensures proper treatment, recycling.

Common Effluent Treatment Plants (CETP)

Centralized effluent treatment for multiple industries. Helps achieve regulatory compliance and minimizes environmental impact.

Sewage Treatment Plants (STP)

Primary & biological treatment to remove pollutants. Prevents water pollution with proper operation and maintenance.

Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP)

Chemical & biological treatment neutralizes pollutants. Regular monitoring ensures compliance with regulations.

Multiple Effect Evaporators (MEE)

Concentrates wastewater and recovers valuable resources. Scale management and safety measures are essential.

Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Membrane cleaning and pressure monitoring to maintain efficiency. Sanitization prevents biofouling and microbial growth.

Zero Liquid Discharge Systems (ZLD)

Advanced treatment system ensuring minimal liquid waste discharge. Combines RO, MEE, and crystallizers for sustainable water recovery.

Softeners

Removes hardness-causing calcium and magnesium ions from water. Ensures scale-free equipment and improves efficiency in industrial applications.

Demineralization Plants (DM)

Produces high-purity water by removing dissolved salts and minerals. Uses ion-exchange technology for reliable and consistent output.

MPCB Consent

Assistance in obtaining consent from Maharashtra Pollution Control Board (MPCB) for establishing and operating industries in compliance with environmental norms.

Ozone System

The Ozone system reduces organic matter, COD/BOD load, disinfects odour & colour, and oxidizes pollutants effectively.

Ultrafiltration (UF)

Separates bacteria, viruses, and colloids with a compact design, stable output, and minimal chemical use.

Dissolved Air Flotation Unit

Efficiently removes suspended solids, oils, and grease through air bubble flotation and skimming.

Primary & Secondary Clarifier

Removes suspended solids using gravity settling with mechanical scrapers for sludge & scum.

Wet Fume Scrubber

Neutralizes corrosive gases, absorbs particulates, and handles high-temperature streams efficiently.

OCEMS

Online Continuous Effluent Monitoring System for real-time water quality tracking and regulatory compliance.

Aquagreen Tech Solutions delivers sustainable innovations in water & wastewater treatment, ensuring a greener and cleaner future for industries and communities.
