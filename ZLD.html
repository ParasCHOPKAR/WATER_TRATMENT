Zero Liquid Discharge
(ZLD) Systems

  • ✔ Complete Waste Elimination: Ensures zero liquid waste discharge, eco-friendly and compliant.
  • ✔ High Recovery: Recovers 95–99% of wastewater as reusable water.
  • ✔ Sustainable: Reduces fresh water usage, converting residues into disposable salts.
  • ✔ Cost-Effective: Long-term savings for industries with strict wastewater norms.

Industries Using ZLD:

  • ✅ Power Plants
  • ✅ Textile & Dyeing Units
  • ✅ Pharmaceutical Industry
  • ✅ Oil & Gas Refineries
  • ✅ Chemical & Fertilizer Plants
