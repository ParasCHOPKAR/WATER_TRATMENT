AMC Services

We offer highly qualified and skilled teams for Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) — both comprehensive and non-comprehensive — tailored for water and wastewater treatment systems. Our expertise covers a broad spectrum of operations, ensuring efficiency, compliance, and sustainability.

Supply of Spares

Provision of high-quality spare parts for water and wastewater treatment systems, ensuring smooth operations, minimal downtime, and adherence to OEM standards. This includes timely replacement, proactive inventory management, and strategic stock planning. All spares are OEM-compliant, covering critical components such as pumps, valves, membranes, sensors, chemical dosing units, and control panels. Regular inspection, predictive maintenance scheduling, and expert technical support guarantee optimal system performance, longevity, and compliance with environmental and safety standards. Cost-effective procurement and quality assurance protocols further enhance reliability and operational efficiency.

24-Hour Operations

Round-the-clock operation monitoring and support for water and wastewater treatment systems, ensuring uninterrupted service, optimal process efficiency, and strict compliance with environmental and safety regulations. Our 24/7 operations team provides real-time system supervision, remote monitoring via SCADA/IoT-enabled platforms, and immediate troubleshooting of faults or alarms. Emergency response protocols are in place for power failures, equipment breakdowns, or process deviations, minimizing downtime and safeguarding treated water quality. Comprehensive reporting and performance tracking are conducted daily to ensure operational transparency, regulatory adherence, and continuous improvement of treatment efficiency.

Supply of Chemicals & Consumables

Continuous supply of high-quality treatment chemicals and consumables for water and wastewater treatment plants, ensuring optimal process efficiency and regulatory compliance. This includes dosing chemicals, coagulants, flocculants, bio-cultures, de-chlorination agents, pH adjusters, and specialized reagents. Our services guarantee timely delivery, proper storage, and inventory management to prevent operational interruptions. Customized chemical dosing programs are provided based on real-time water quality data to optimize treatment performance, minimize chemical wastage, and reduce operational costs. Regular quality checks and batch testing ensure that all chemicals meet industry standards and support long-term plant reliability.

Operation Services & Maintenance

Comprehensive operation and maintenance (O&M) services for water and wastewater treatment plants, covering preventive maintenance, real-time monitoring, system optimization, and predictive troubleshooting. Our team conducts routine inspections, calibrates equipment, cleans filters and membranes, and ensures all pumps, valves, and sensors function at peak efficiency. Detailed performance reports are provided, including process parameters, chemical consumption, and energy usage, enabling data-driven decision-making. Emergency response protocols ensure minimal downtime, while optimization strategies improve plant efficiency, reduce operational costs, and extend equipment lifespan. Tailored maintenance schedules and proactive recommendations help industries comply with environmental norms and achieve sustainable water management.

Partial Operation & Maintenance Service

Limited preventive maintenance and operational support tailored to the client’s selected water or wastewater treatment systems. This service includes scheduled inspections, basic equipment checks, calibration of critical components, and troubleshooting of commonly occurring issues. Ideal for organizations preferring partial coverage to reduce costs, it ensures essential system functionality while allowing clients to manage non-critical operations independently. Our team provides guidance on chemical dosing, operational best practices, and compliance requirements, helping industries maintain regulatory standards, reduce unexpected downtime, and extend the life of key equipment. Flexible service schedules can be adjusted based on seasonal or operational demands.

Technical Support on Demand

On-demand technical assistance for troubleshooting, optimization, and expert guidance on water and wastewater treatment systems. This service allows facilities to access our skilled engineers whenever required, without a full-time contract. It includes remote and on-site support for system diagnostics, process optimization, chemical dosing guidance, emergency response, and minor repairs. Ideal for organizations that manage spare parts and day-to-day operations internally, it ensures compliance with environmental regulations, enhances operational efficiency, reduces downtime, and provides actionable recommendations to prolong equipment life. Flexible response times and customized support plans make this solution cost-effective while maintaining high-quality standards.
