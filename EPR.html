Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

We provide complete assistance in EPR Registration from CPCB for Plastic Waste, E-Waste, Battery Waste, and Tyre Waste Producers. Our experts ensure your business stays compliant with CPCB regulations while contributing to a cleaner and greener future.

EPR for Plastic Waste

Registration of PIBOs (Producers, Importers, Brand Owners) & Plastic Waste Processors (PWPs) as per CPCB guidelines. We assist in online registration, quarterly & annual returns, and compliance audits.

  • ✅ PIBO / PWP Registration – Assistance with registration of Producers, Importers & Brand Owners (PIBOs) and Plastic Waste Processors (PWPs) under the Plastic Waste Management (PWM) Rules, 2016 & amendments. Ensures compliance with Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)
  • ✅ Plastic Waste Annual Returns – Preparation and filing of mandatory annual returns related to plastic packaging, recycling, and disposal. Includes documentation of plastic generated, collected, processed, and end-of-life management. Helps industries demonstrate compliance with EPR targets and maintain transparency with regulatory authorities.
  • ✅ Compliance Documentation – End-to-end support in maintaining required records, including EPR Action Plans, agreements with recyclers/co-processors, waste tracking documents, and statutory forms. Ensures readiness for audits, inspections, and regulatory reporting while reducing the risk of penalties and non-compliance issues.

EPR for E-Waste

E-Waste management as per E-Waste Management Rules, 2022. We handle registration, channelization of e-waste, and recycling certificates for compliance.

  • ✅ CPCB E-Waste Producer Registration – Assistance in obtaining registration of Producers, Importers & Manufacturers of electrical and electronic equipment (EEE) under the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2016 & amendments. Ensures compliance with Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) obligations laid down by the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB). This includes preparation of EPR Plans, collection targets, and channelization of e-waste through authorized mechanisms.
  • ✅ Annual & Quarterly Filing – Preparation and submission of mandatory quarterly and annual compliance returns detailing the quantity of EEE placed on the market, e-waste collected, recycled, and disposed. Filing is done as per CPCB/SPCB requirements to demonstrate compliance with EPR targets, waste collection, and recycling obligations. Proper filing protects industries from penalties and cancellation of registration.
  • ✅ Authorized Recycler Tie-Ups – Facilitation of agreements with CPCB/SPCB authorized e-waste recyclers, dismantlers, and collection centers. Ensures safe disposal and recycling of hazardous e-waste fractions such as PCBs, heavy metals, plastics, and glass components. We provide documentation of recycling certificates, ensuring transparency, traceability, and compliance with environmental norms.

EPR for Battery Waste

Assistance with Battery Waste Management Rules, 2022 compliance. Covers lithium-ion, lead-acid, and other types of batteries under the EPR framework.

  • ✅ CPCB Battery EPR Registration – Assistance in obtaining Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) Authorization from the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) under the Battery Waste Management Rules, 2022. This applies to manufacturers, importers, assemblers, and re-conditioners of batteries (automotive, industrial, portable, and e-vehicle batteries). We support in preparation of EPR plans, recycling obligations, and digital portal registrations.
  • ✅ Extended Compliance Support – End-to-end guidance for meeting EPR obligations, collection targets, and recycling milestones as defined in the CPCB guidelines. This includes quarterly & annual return filing, record-keeping of waste batteries collected, refurbished, or recycled, and submission of compliance evidence (invoices, recycling certificates, acknowledgments from recyclers).
  • ✅ Recycler / Collection Center Coordination – Facilitation of tie-ups with CPCB/SPCB authorized recyclers, refurbishers, and collection centers to ensure environmentally sound management of used batteries. We ensure safe channelization, proper documentation, and certificate collection for batteries recycled, reused, or disposed, maintaining full compliance with Battery Waste Management Rules and avoiding regulatory penalties.

EPR for Tyre Waste

Compliance support for Tyre Waste Management, ensuring producers & importers meet EPR obligations through authorized recyclers.

  • ✅ Tyre Producer / Importer Registration – Assistance in obtaining Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) Authorization for tyre manufacturers, importers, and re-conditioners under the Plastic Waste Management Rules & Tyre Waste Management Guidelines. Includes preparation of EPR plans, portal registration with SPCB/CPCB, and guidance on statutory obligations.
  • ✅ Waste Tyre Disposal Plans – Development of environmentally sound tyre waste management plans that ensure collection, transportation, storage, and recycling or disposal in compliance with CPCB/SPCB norms. Plans include recycling targets, co-processing in cement kilns, pyrolysis, and safe landfill options.
  • ✅ Compliance Reporting – End-to-end support for filing annual/quarterly reports regarding tyre collection, recycling, or disposal. Ensures proper documentation, record-keeping, and submission of compliance certificates to authorities, avoiding regulatory penalties. Includes coordination with authorized recyclers and collection centers for traceability.
